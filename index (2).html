Our Story

Baked from the Heart,
Served with Soul

La Farine was born in 1998 from a small family kitchen in Lahore. Our founder, Chef Amara, dreamed of bringing the warmth of French artisan baking to Pakistan — without compromising on tradition or quality.

Every morning at 4 AM, our bakers arrive to begin the day's work. No shortcuts, no preservatives, no compromises. Just flour, water, salt, time, and a lifetime of passion.
